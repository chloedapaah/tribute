Virgil Abloh

Virgil Abloh was an influential fashion designer and entrepreneur. A trained architect, Abloh entered the fashion world in 2009 with an internship at Fendi, along with rapper Kanye West. He then became a creative director at Donda in 2010. In 2013, Abloh founded Off-White, an Itaian luxury fashion label. In 2018, Abloh was named as one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time magazine following his accomplishment as the first African American artistic director at a French luxury fashion house when he joined LMVH. Bridging streetwear and luxury clothing, Abloh became a huge inspirational figure and managed to reach a level of global fame unusual for a designer.
